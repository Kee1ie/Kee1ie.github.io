My Projects

Kaizen Trio Recital

At the School of Music, I rehearse and perform regularly with violinist Alexandre Negrao and cellist Dean Wibe as the Kaizen Piano Trio. We performed in the Kauffman Center in Kansas City for a masterclass with Michael Stern and were finalists in the 2024 National Music Teachers National Association (MTNA) Chamber Music Competition. The Kaizen Trio put on a recital on April 12th, 2024 at Sheryl Crow Hall. In this concert, we performed Grieg's Andante con moto, Gabriela Lena Frank's Four Folk Songs for Piano Trio, and Ravel’s Piano Trio.

Senior Degree Recital

This recital was performed on December 2nd, 2023, as a part of my degree program, which requires me to perform at least two full-length recitals. The concert was held in Sheryl Crow Hall and livestreamed by the School of Music. The program consisted of Bach’s Toccata in D Minor, Beethoven’s Sonata No. 28 in A Major, Chopin’s Scherzo No. 4 in E Major, Chopin’s Etude No.7 in C Major, Liszt’s Paganini Etude No. 2, and Stravinsky’s Firebird Suite (arranged by Guido Agosti). Bowdoin International Music Festival

During the summer of 2023, I attended the Bowdoin International Music Festival held in Brunswick, Maine. The festival is prestigious, with music students from all over the world attending. I love getting to meet different musicians from all kinds of backgrounds. For three weeks, I studied solo repertoire with pianist Matti Raekallio and chamber music with pianist Liza Stepanova. In this short time, I rehearsed and performed two movements of Smetana’s Piano Trio in G Minor in the Studzinski Recital Hall. I was also able to attend many faculty concerts, where there was an emphasis on underrepresented pieces.
