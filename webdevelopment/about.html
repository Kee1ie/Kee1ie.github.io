Ryan Kee

My name is Ryan Kee, and I am an aspiring concert pianist.

I am a senior at the University of Missouri - Columbia and will graduate in Spring 2024. My major is Piano Performance, and I work extensively in the School of Music. I plan to receive a business minor, an IT certificate, and a multicultural studies certificate.

After graduating, I intend to continue my education in graduate school. Along with performing, I love teaching students through the university’s Community Music Program. I have had success in various solo and chamber music competitions. Most recently, my trio was a finalist in the 2024 National Music Teachers National Association (MTNA) Chamber Music Competition. As I build up my professional career, I hope to inspire others in my community through music.

“To send light into the darkness of men's hearts – such is the duty of the artist.” -Robert Schumann
